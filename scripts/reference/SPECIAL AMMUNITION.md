SPECIAL AMMUNITION
Most weapons allow for the use of Special Ammunition, which is
customized ammo with special properties selected by the User.
Special Ammunition can be combined with other Compatible types
allowing for powerful combinations. There are separate tables for
Standard Ammunition, Shotgun Ammunition, Flamethrower Fuels,
Cryosprayer Fuels, 40mm Grenades, and Explosives. Each Custom
Ammunition Type has a Name, Damage, Pierce, Compatibility,
Abilities, and a Cost.
The amount of Special Ammunition that can be placed on a
single round is up to 4 total options, not including Tracer, Over-
Pressurized, Under-Pressurized, Cold Load, and Match Grade. For
example, a User can have AP, HE, IN, and HV on a single round. They
could then also make it Match Grade without it counting as going over
4 options on the Special Ammunition.
Shotgun Slugs can’t be combined. A Shotgun Slug will
always be a standalone option. The only exception to this is the Rifled
Slug Special Ammunition. The Rifled Slug is a special modifier that can
only be applied to Slugs and can’t be taken alone.
SPECIAL AMMUNITION: DAMAGE AND PIERCE
Damage and Pierce will show the amount Damage added or
subtracted from the Weapon it’s being applied to. Damage can be
either Damage Dice or Base Damage modified. Damage Dice, Base
Damage, and Pierce can’t go below 0.
SPECIAL AMMUNITION: COMPATIBILITY
Compatibility will list the other Custom Ammunition options that it is
compatible with. For example, Armor Piercing lists that it is
compatible with High-Explosive and Incendiary, while Incendiary lists
it is also compatible High-Explosive. This means you can make
APHEIN, which is Armor Piercing, High-Explosive, Incendiary Rounds.
You will add up all of the devious Damage, Pierce, Ability, and Price
modifications together to create your new ammunition.
Custom Ammo compatibility that is listed as “None” can’t
be used with any other type of custom ammunition, regardless of
what is listed by other ammo compatibility.
Custom Ammo compatibility options that list “All” will work
with any Ammo customization option, regardless of what it lists,
except for those with Compatibility listed as “None.”
SPECIAL AMMUNITION: ABILITY
Special Ammunition may sometimes list an Ability, which is a special
modifier to a Weapon that is being used. This may cover a Special
Rule, a change in a Weapon’s Range, or adding a new effect to an
Attack. Abilities will stack with other Abilities unless specified
otherwise. However, if something lists that it removes a Special Rule,
while another lists that it adds the Special Rule, the Ammunition
Ability that removes the Special Rule will override it.
SPECIAL AMMUNITION: COST
Special Ammunition has a listed multiplier for the Cost. You will take
the multipliers of all chosen Special Ammunition and add them
together. This total multiplier is then used to multiply the Cost of the
Ammunition you’re buying it with.
Any Special Ammunition entry that has a Cost of “--” will not
add to the total multiplier of Special Ammunition. This means that this
Special Ammunition is free to take.
For example, if it costs 10 cR for 100 rounds of 7.62 you can
buy 10 rounds for 1 cR. If the multiplier for your chosen Special
Ammunition is x15, it will cost 15 cR for those 10 rounds.
There are instances of Special Ammunition which do not
have a multiplier. These are for Tanks, which are purchased in sets of
one.
SNIPER ROUNDS, SHOTGUN SHELLS, AND CHEMICAL SPRAYER FUEL
Shotguns and Flamethrowers can only be used alongside their own
respective Customization charts. Flamethrowers also have access to
the Flamethrower Special, which can be taken in addition to anything
else selected in the Flamethrower Customization chart.
Rifles, Sniper Rifles, and Machine Guns that have the Long
Barrel Special Rule can make use of the Grappling Round. A unique
option for long-barreled weapons to fire off usable grappling hooks.
40MM GRENADES
The 40mm Grenade list aren’t modifiers or customization options.
These entries are each their own type of 40mm Grenade and gives
the statistics for each possible choice.
You are unable to modify 40mm Grenades using the Missile,
Rocket, and Cannon Shell Customization table.
